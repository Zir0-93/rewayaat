About HDP

The objective of the Hadith Database Project (HDP) is to make accessible to the English speaking community a wide range of Hadiths in an easily searchable format. Currently there are over 33,000 Hadiths stored on the database. Since the aim of the project was to provide easy access to the Hadith rather than focus on their authenticity, a reference to the sanad (chain of narrators) of the Hadith is not always provided.
